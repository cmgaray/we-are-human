Remove Trump: Defend the Constitution

Donald Trump has repeatedly violated the US Constitution and shown clear authoritarian and fascist tendencies:

  • Inciting an insurrection (January 6th, 2021)
  • Attempting to overturn democratic elections
  • Threatening press freedom and attacking judicial independence
  • Separating families and violating human rights through ICE policies
  • Calling for political violence and revenge campaigns against opponents

This is a constitutional emergency. Trump is a threat to democracy. Demand his disqualification and removal from public office under the 14th Amendment, Section 3.
